# Client Brief Summary

## Engagement Framing

This engagement should be treated as a Consulting Engagement with Agency Engagement implications, not as a candidate application response.

The job posting is used as source material to infer the commercial business problem behind the role. The strategic question is not whether a candidate fits the role. The question is how Syneos Health can scale market research and analytics into a clearer commercial operating model.

## Client Hypothesis

Syneos Health appears to need a more repeatable insight engine for pharmaceutical and biotech client work. The role description points to a need for integrated research inputs, executive-ready recommendations, KPI tracking, and cross-functional alignment.

## Expected Business Problem

The likely problem is operational, not cosmetic:

- insight work is probably spread across multiple methods and teams
- executive decision-making likely needs a more standardized reporting layer
- customer segmentation and market intelligence likely need to be more repeatable
- the organization likely needs a stronger connection between research, analytics, and commercial action

## What This Engagement Should Produce

The engagement should produce a directional commercial strategy package that covers:

- market research operating model
- customer intelligence framework
- analytics maturity
- measurement framework
- executive reporting
- voice of customer
- competitive intelligence
- 90-day discovery plan
- 12-month analytics roadmap
- risks, assumptions, and open questions

## Source Basis

The Syneos job posting emphasizes integrated insights, voice-of-customer work, KPI dashboards, cross-functional partnership, and 30/60/90-day expectations. Syneos’ public site also emphasizes AI-driven performance and predictive commercial solutions, which supports the inference that this is an operating model and decision-quality problem, not just an individual role fill.
