# Strategy Scorecard

| Area | Current Hypothesis | Target State | Priority | Confidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Market research operating model | Strong effort, possibly fragmented workflow | Repeatable insight workflow with clear handoffs | High | Medium |
| Customer intelligence | Useful but probably uneven by segment | Reusable segmentation and stakeholder intelligence framework | High | Medium |
| Analytics maturity | Moderate-to-advanced, but not fully standardized | Clear KPI definitions, cadence, and ownership | High | Medium |
| Executive reporting | Likely produced, but not fully systemized | Repeatable executive dashboard and decision memo layer | High | Medium |
| Voice of customer | Present, but likely not always reused | Standing VOC input to planning and segmentation | High | Medium |
| Competitive intelligence | Likely collected episodically | Operationalized competitive intelligence workflow | Medium | Medium |
| Decision speed | Constrained by synthesis and alignment | Faster, cleaner commercial decisions | High | Medium |

## Readout

The highest-priority gap is not data volume. It is the operating model that turns data into action.

If Syneos can make research, analytics, VOC, and executive reporting repeatable, the business should get better decision quality with less manual friction.
