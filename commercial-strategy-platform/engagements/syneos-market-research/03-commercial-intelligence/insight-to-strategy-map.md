# Insight To Strategy Map

| Discovery signal | Strategic interpretation | Recommended response | Confidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Integrated research inputs are explicitly required | The current insight workflow likely spans multiple methods and data sources | Build a repeatable intake and synthesis workflow | High |
| Executive-ready recommendations are required | Leadership needs insights that are decision-oriented, not descriptive | Standardize the executive narrative and recommendation format | High |
| KPI dashboards and performance trackers are called out | Measurement likely needs clearer ownership and cadence | Define a shared KPI framework and reporting rhythm | High |
| Voice-of-customer work is part of the role | VOC may exist, but probably needs a more reusable operating system | Make VOC a standing input to segmentation and planning | High |
| Stakeholder alignment is a recurring requirement | Insight work likely stalls between research and execution | Build a cross-functional decision cadence | Medium |
| AI and advanced analytics appear in the public story | Technology is part of the value proposition, but the operating model still needs discipline | Pair AI use with clear business questions and governance | Medium |
| 30/60/90-day expectations emphasize rapid ramp and action | The business likely wants near-term clarity before broader transformation | Start with a focused discovery and quick wins plan | High |
