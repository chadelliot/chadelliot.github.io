# Assumptions

1. The job posting is a proxy for a broader commercial need, not only a staffing gap.
2. Syneos wants to improve decision quality across client-facing commercial work.
3. The current research and analytics environment is likely fragmented across methods, tools, or teams.
4. Executive stakeholders need more standardized reporting than the current workflow provides.
5. The client-facing opportunity is about scaling repeatable insight, not producing isolated research artifacts.
6. The most valuable first use case is likely a narrower, high-priority client segment or therapeutic area.
7. AI and data tooling are relevant, but the core issue is operating model clarity.
8. A 90-day discovery plan should come before any broad implementation roadmap.
