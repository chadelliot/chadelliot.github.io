# Commercial Discovery Summary

## What The Discovery Says

This engagement is best understood as a commercial operating model problem.

Syneos Health appears to need a more scalable insight system that can connect research, analytics, customer understanding, and executive decision-making. The role posting is explicit about integrated data sources, executive-ready narratives, KPI dashboards, and cross-functional partnership. That is enough to support a directional strategy even without pretending to know the internal org design.

## Strongest Signals

- Syneos publicly positions itself around commercialization, consulting, and AI-enabled commercial performance.
- The role asks for integration across qualitative, quantitative, syndicated, and advanced analytics inputs.
- The role expects KPI dashboards, performance trackers, and leadership-ready recommendations.
- The 30/60/90-day expectations imply an urgent need to identify gaps, launch priorities, and standardize cadence.

## Strategic Read

The likely business problem is not just "more research."

It is:

- a fragmented research and analytics workflow
- inconsistent translation from evidence to action
- weak standardization of executive reporting
- inconsistent customer intelligence across segments and stakeholders
- a need for a repeatable voice-of-customer and competitive intelligence process

## What Needs Validation

- Which teams own research, analytics, and reporting today
- Whether the role is supporting client work, internal enablement, or both
- Which segments or therapeutic areas are the immediate priority
- Which KPIs leadership already trusts
- Whether the biggest gap is process, tooling, or talent

## Bottom Line

The right engagement frame is consulting and agency-style strategy support, not a candidate response. The output should help Syneos clarify the market research and analytics operating model it needs to support commercial decision-making at scale.
