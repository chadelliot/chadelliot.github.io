# Open Questions

## Operating Model

- Who owns market research and analytics today?
- How are qualitative, quantitative, syndicated, and advanced analytics inputs coordinated?
- What decisions are blocked by the current operating model?

## Scope

- Is the work primarily internal, client-facing, or both?
- Which commercial teams are most dependent on this function?
- Which therapeutic areas or client segments are most urgent?

## Measurement

- Which KPIs do executives already trust?
- What dashboards exist today?
- What leading indicators matter most for commercial decisions?

## Voice Of Customer

- How is HCP, patient, and payer insight currently collected and reused?
- How is competitive intelligence captured and shared?
- Where does insight get lost between research and action?

## Roadmap

- What should be proven in the first 90 days?
- What can wait until the 12-month roadmap?
- What would make the leadership team call this engagement successful?
