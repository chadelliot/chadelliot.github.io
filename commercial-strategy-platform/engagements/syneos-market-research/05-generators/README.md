# Generators

## Purpose

This folder defines the future generator layer for the Syneos engagement workspace.

The generators should consume the Commercial Strategy Model only. They should not perform independent research, reinterpret the job posting, or bypass the commercial discovery and intelligence layers.

## Flow

```text
Client Brief
Commercial Discovery
Commercial Intelligence
Commercial Strategy Model
Generators
Deliverables
```

## Rule

One engagement can produce many deliverables, but it should keep one upstream strategy model.

The generator layer should vary tone, packaging, and output format. It should not vary the underlying commercial logic.

## Supported Generator Targets

- Executive summary
- Commercial strategy
- Market research operating model
- Analytics roadmap
- Executive dashboard
- Statement of work

## Operating Standard

For this Syneos engagement, the generator layer should stay directional, practical, and executive-ready. It should emphasize operating model clarity, measurement, and decision support rather than candidate framing.
