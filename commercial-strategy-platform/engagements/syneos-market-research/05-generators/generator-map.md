# Generator Map

| Deliverable | Primary audience | Purpose | Primary inputs | Output form |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Executive summary | Executive sponsor | Compress the engagement into a concise decision memo | Business problem, objectives, risks, recommendations | One-page summary |
| Commercial strategy | Commercial leadership | Show the end-to-end strategic view | Audience architecture, intelligence, measurement, roadmap | Strategy brief |
| Market research operating model | Insights and analytics leaders | Define the research workflow and governance model | Operating model, VOC, intelligence workflow | Operating model document |
| Analytics roadmap | Leadership and program owners | Sequence capability building across 12 months | Analytics maturity, roadmap, assumptions | Roadmap document |
| Executive dashboard | Executive sponsors | Define the KPIs and cadence leadership should inspect | Measurement framework, leading and lagging indicators | Dashboard spec |
| Statement of work | Procurement and delivery sponsors | Define scope, milestones, assumptions, and boundaries | Objectives, deliverables, risks, open questions | SOW document |

## Notes

- Generators should reuse the same strategy model.
- Generators should not request new research.
- Generators may narrow the content to their audience and format.
