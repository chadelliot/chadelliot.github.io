# 09 Discovery Workshop Generator

## Purpose

Generate a discovery workshop plan that uses the Opportunity Model to guide structured learning, validation, and alignment.

## Primary Audience

- Executive Hire: hiring team or future stakeholders in a strategic working session
- Consulting Engagement: client sponsor, functional leaders, implementation stakeholders
- Agency Engagement: client marketing, revenue, sales, or account stakeholders

## Supported Engagement Types

- Executive Hire
- Consulting Engagement
- Agency Engagement

## Required Opportunity Model Inputs

- `opportunity.engagementType`
- `opportunity.primaryAudience`
- `company`
- `industry`
- `businessModel`
- `objectives`
- `commercialChallenges`
- `growthOpportunities`
- `audience`
- `segmentation`
- `tam`
- `personas`
- `journey`
- `signals`
- `measurement`
- `technology`
- `risks`
- `assumptions`
- `recommendations`

## Optional Inputs

- attendee list
- workshop duration
- current-state materials
- open questions
- known constraints
- desired decisions
- facilitation format

## Dependencies

- Opportunity Model
- engagement type contract
- research checklist
- writing style guide

## Output Format

Workshop agenda with objectives, pre-read, discussion blocks, discovery questions, exercises, decisions, and follow-up outputs.

## Writing Style

Practical, structured, curious, and facilitative. Use questions to validate assumptions rather than presenting internal unknowns as facts.

## Validation Rules

- The generator does not perform research.
- It must identify which assumptions need validation.
- It must include discovery questions tied to the model.
- It must avoid overloading the workshop with too many objectives.
- Engagement type determines facilitation posture and deliverables.

## Success Criteria

- The workshop helps validate the strategy against internal constraints.
- Participants understand the decisions needed.
- Outputs can update the Opportunity Model.
- The agenda is practical for the allotted time.

## Example Output Structure

```text
Workshop objective
Participants
Pre-read
Agenda
Discovery questions
Validation exercises
Decisions needed
Follow-up outputs
Model updates
```
