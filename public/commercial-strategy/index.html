Chapter 02 — The Growth Question

What are we growing? And for whom?

Chapter 06 — Customer Value Tiering

Every customer has a next move.

For current customers, the goal is not only retention. The goal is expansion. We use customer value, purchase behavior, product fit, and growth potential to determine which customers can move up — and what motion is required to move them.

"We use customer value, purchase behavior, product fit, and growth potential to understand which customers can move from Tier 3 to Tier 2, or from Tier 2 to Tier 1."
T1
Highest-Value Customers
Largest revenue, broadest category depth, strongest relationship. Protect and grow at all costs.
  • Executive relationship management
  • Strategic account plans
  • Loyalty & retention campaigns
  • Proactive cross-sell by seller
  • Early access to new products
T2
Growth Customers
Good fit, solid revenue, underpenetrated category. Clear path to Tier 1 with the right motion.
  • Category expansion campaigns
  • Personalized cross-sell offers
  • Seller-assisted growth plays
  • Lifecycle nurture sequences
  • Reorder reminders, usage signals
T3
Underdeveloped Customers
Low purchase frequency or narrow category use. Relationship exists but full potential is unrealized.
  • Re-engagement campaigns
  • Product education content
  • Category introduction sequences
  • Automated nurture tracks
  • Win-back with incentive
