Chapter 02 — Segmentation

Where Should We Compete?

Commercial segmentation identifies where incremental investment is most likely to create profitable growth. Rather than treating every customer equally, we prioritize the markets that create the highest enterprise value.

Executive Recommendation
Increase investment toward Enterprise and Growth segments, where expansion revenue, strategic account value, and long-term commercial opportunity are highest. Maintain efficient acquisition in Mid-Market while selectively investing in Emerging opportunities.
